Our Instructors

Meet Master Dan

A 4th Degree Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do with over 25 years expierence Master Dan Mitchell is the leader of all Tae Kwon Do classes. He has been teaching for 8 years after earning certifications as a self defense instructor and Kickboxing instructor. He also holds certifications in Tactical Self Defense, and as a Personal Protector in addition to earning the rank of Master from the World Tae Kwon Do Federation. As if that wasn't enough he is also a 5th degree black belt in Moo Duk Kwan and a Black Belt in Hapkido. His favorite kick is the 540 backspin.

Meet Group Coach Sam

A Glencoe native and Special Education teacher began tacking Kick Boxing in 2016 and was hooked. She has since joined TaeKwonDo and has advanced to her green belt. In order to improve her life style and help others improve their health Sam is currently earning her certification as a Kettlebell instructor; Yoga instructor, and Master Wellness Coach. "I hope to bring new oppurtunities for health and movement to the community."
